Armored War Assault II Lite Instructions:

Player 1 (orange) use Left or Right Arrow Keys to move in the left or right direction.

Use Up or Down Arrow Keys to increase or decrease the angle of the tank cannon relative to the ground.

Press X to end your turn.

Player 2 (white) use A or D Keys to move in the left or right direction.

Use W or S Keys to increase or decrease the angle of the tank cannon relative to the ground.

Press M to end your turn.

Player 1 cannot move while Player 2 is moving and vice versa.
